About Me

Before i start i'd like to say i hope everyone is having a good day at GP and otherwise and i believe we can make it better if anything in my thread interests you ^^

I consider myself fairly new to writing in general perhaps not so much in role playing as i've dabbled in it verbally as a child if that counts ^^ but as a writer im learning better ways to express myself and i feel like i improve with every plot i write or every reply i follow up with.

Over the time i've found out that i prefer playing female characters over male characters in RP's as that is what really allows my creativity to flow, that is not to say however that i don't play male characters i certainly do! but as of now i have way more plots where the main character is a female than a male that's only the case for my own plots however im more up for developing one with an interested partner and what gender i play wouldn't be an issue there.

Preferences & Expectations


Time & Frequency of replies - This is where i do not have any specific expectations from my partner all i ask is that they reply whether its once a week or once a day, just let me know if you feel like the RP wont work don't keep me hanging! ^^ as far as what you can expect from me in terms of replies i usually write back once a day or once every other day however it is entirely dependent on my real life schedule so at times it could be even longer or even faster but i.ll always make sure to let you know whichever is the case.

General expectations from my partner - The single most important i ask of you is to be involved in the story we play whether its one of my plots or your's or one we build together i want you to try to be as creative and interactive in the RP as i would be. By that i mean i would prefer a reply that gives me more than just answering my previous reply, it gives me something new to build on so it makes it easier for me to progress the story in my next reply. In simple terms add your own plot twists! they are fun and move the story along just as you would expect me to do so i'd expect the same from you so don't be shy to step out of the line and add what you like simply because its "my plot" i.ll let you know if it doesn't work but you shouldn't be worried otherwise! ^^

All in all i am looking more for a partner that's interested in storytelling then just a plot that leads to some smut scene there will be stories where our characters might not even meet for a fair amount of time and as such it would be extremely important for you to pour in your creativity to bring your side of the story to life while i bring mine to life if that makes sense? i hope it does!. I aim to create a story with you that is memorable one that you have poured your heart into. It can indeed contain mature theme's but it won't ever really be the main focus of it unless explicitly stated.
